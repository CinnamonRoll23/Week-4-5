The Thress Idiots

In college, Farhan and Raju form a great bond with Rancho due to his positive and refreshing outlook to life. Years later, a bet gives them a chance to look for their long-lost friend whose existence seems rather elusive.

Rajkumar Hirani

Rajkumar "Raju" Hirani is an Indian filmmaker known for his works in Hindi films. He is the recipient of several accolades, including four National Film Awards and 11 Filmfare Awards. Hirani is referred as one of the most successful filmmakers of Indian cinema.


Sypnosis

The film circles around the dominant themes such as parental expectations, Student choices, teacher's traditional attitudes towards learning, societal influences on the student's educational development, and creative learning vis-a-vis rote learning.


The plot of "The Three Idiots

Two friends are searching for their long lost companion. They revisit their college days and recall the memories of their friend who inspired them to think differently, even as the rest of the world called them "idiots".


Setting

IIM Bangalore

The fictitious Imperial College of Engineering (ICE) is the site where most of the interesting scenes in the movie were filmed.


The main protagonist of the story


Aamir Khan. as Ranchoddas `Rancho` Shyamaldas Chanchad / Phunsukh Wangdu

He is kind and he cares for the well-being of his friends and others around him, including Dr. Sahastrabuddhe's daughters Mona and Pia, the latter of whom was stuck in an engagement with a materialistic banker named Suhas. Besides his brilliant philosophy, Rancho is also an optimistic and passionate student.



Sharman Joshi. as Raju Rastogi

Raju Rastogi is introduced as a hardworking and sincere student who hails from a modest background. Coming from a lower-income family, he bears the heavy responsibility of securing a promising career to alleviate the financial burdens faced by his parents.



R. Madhavan. as Farhan Qureshi

Farhan had continuously tried to live life by following his parents expectations throughout his college years. But still, it is also observed that Farhan is an intelligent and creative individual, who appreciates life and nature around him, and captures every moment on camera.
